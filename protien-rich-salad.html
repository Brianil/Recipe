Protien-rich-salad

Ingredients

  • Soya chunk

  • Raddish
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Green chilli

Steps

  1. This is a lazy recipe. Collect all the Ingredients in appropriate amount which you want to eat.
  2. Chop, slice, cut, shred do whatever you prefer.

  4. Add curd and mix.
  5. serve when hungry.
